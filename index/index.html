Turmeric Rhizome

Turmeric rhizome, derived from the Curcuma longa plant, contains curcuminoids, primarily curcumin, known for their potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. In CelluCare, turmeric rhizome helps support healthy blood sugar levels by improving insulin sensitivity and reducing insulin resistance. Curcumin also helps mitigate inflammation linked to metabolic disorders, thereby supporting overall metabolic health and reducing the risk of chronic diseases associated with inflammation.

Eleuthero

Eleuthero, also known as Siberian ginseng, is an adaptogenic herb known for its ability to increase resilience to stress and improve energy levels. In CelluCare, eleuthero helps combat fatigue and enhances physical and mental stamina, making it beneficial for maintaining energy levels throughout the day. By supporting adrenal gland function and enhancing overall vitality, eleuthero contributes to improved endurance and stress management.

Cocoa Bean Extract

Cocoa beans, the seeds of Theobroma cacao, are rich in flavonoids, particularly epicatechin and catechin, which have potent antioxidant properties. These compounds contribute to cardiovascular health by promoting vasodilation, improving blood flow, and reducing the risk of cardiovascular diseases such as hypertension. In CelluCare, cocoa bean extract enhances circulation, supports heart function, and ensures efficient nutrient and oxygen delivery throughout the body, thereby promoting overall vitality and well-being.

Gymnema

Gymnema, derived from Gymnema sylvestre, is traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine for its role in managing blood sugar levels. In CelluCare, gymnema supports healthy blood sugar metabolism by improving insulin function and reducing sugar absorption in the intestines. It also promotes regeneration of insulin-producing beta cells in the pancreas, thereby supporting long-term metabolic health and reducing the risk of diabetes complications. Additionally, gymnema aids in weight management by reducing cravings for sweets and supporting a balanced appetite.

Juniper Berry

Juniper berries, from the Juniperus communis tree, contain bioactive compounds like terpenes and flavonoids that contribute to their diuretic and cardiovascular benefits. In CelluCare, juniper berry supports heart health by promoting diuresis, which helps reduce excess fluid retention and lowers blood pressure. By enhancing kidney function and reducing fluid buildup, juniper berry supports optimal cardiovascular function and overall heart health.

Pine Bark Extract

Pine bark extract, derived from the bark of maritime pine trees (Pinus pinaster), contains powerful antioxidants known as proanthocyanidins. These antioxidants help neutralize free radicals and reduce oxidative stress throughout the body. In CelluCare, pine bark extract supports overall health by reducing chronic inflammation, enhancing immune function, and protecting cells from oxidative damage. It also promotes cardiovascular health by improving blood circulation and supporting vascular integrity.

Banaba Leaf

Banaba leaf, from the Lagerstroemia speciosa tree, contains corosolic acid, a compound known for its anti-diabetic properties. In CelluCare, banaba leaf supports digestive health by promoting healthy glucose metabolism and insulin sensitivity. It helps regulate blood sugar levels by facilitating glucose uptake into cells and reducing glucose absorption in the intestines. Banaba leaf also supports heart health by improving lipid profiles and reducing oxidative stress, contributing to overall cardiovascular wellness.

Butchers Broom

Butcher’s broom, derived from the Ruscus aculeatus plant, contains ruscogenins, steroidal saponins known for their vasoconstrictive and anti-inflammatory effects. In CelluCare, butcher’s broom supports vascular health by strengthening blood vessel walls and enhancing blood flow. It reduces swelling and discomfort in the legs associated with chronic venous insufficiency, promoting optimal circulation and alleviating symptoms such as leg cramps and varicose veins.
